About

WeiLend is a decentralized, non-profit, open-source p2p lending platform that can be extended to create and connect with any token or share system you can imagine.

What if p2p lending contributions could be turned into contractually backed loan shares or custom tokens? Where support for a company, project or idea can be converted into real digital assets. WeiLend makes possible, all this and more. A free, open-source and secure platform to get crowdlending finance your projects within the Ethereum ecosystem.

Crowdlending

Start, discover, operate and contribute to crowdlending campaigns.

Token and Share Systems

Turn loan contributions into real digital loan shares, tokens or what ever you can imagine.

Our Philosophy

Make p2p lending as open, secure and extendable as possible.
